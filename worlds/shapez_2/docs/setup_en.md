# shapez 2

## PLEASE READ BEFORE WONDERING ABOUT ANYTHING

This first version is just a proof of concept. It is playable, but there is very little content for now. I originally wanted to wait for official mod support, but as it will probably be a while until we get that, I looked up what has already been done regarding modding and just tried to also make something. And now, we can officially say that AP is one of the first mods for this game (it's the third on Thunderstore, and there have been mods in the closed-alpha phase of the game, but those are no longer working).

My plan is definitely to continue working on this and actually implement everything from the main gameplay (Milestones, research, unlocking building, etc.) from time to time. However, **make sure to disable automatic updates for the game** as I cannot guarantee that updates do not break everything (and I can't guarantee to publish a patch instantly).

The recommended way of installing this mod is via the r2modman mod manager. You can of course alternatively install BepInEx (5.4.2100) manually and then put the .dll files inside the zip into the BepInEx/plugins directory in your shapez 2 installation. See the configs of this mod for how to enter the connection details. And tell me what you think about the yaml options. (Also credits to qwint for helping me choosing the name)
