# Void Sols

## Where is the options page?

The [player options page for this game](../player-options) contains all the options you need to configure and export a config file.

## What does randomization do to this game?

In Void Sols, randomization affects various items and equipment found throughout the world.
Players will find weapons, upgrades, and other essential items in randomized locations.

## What is the goal of Void Sols when randomized?

The primary goal is to reach the Apex and interact with the World Spark after defeating various bosses throughout the world.

## What items and locations get shuffled?

- **Weapons**: Various weapons like Swords, Daggers, Great Hammers, etc.
- **Sparks of Light**: If enabled, these can count as checks.
- **Torches**: If enabled, these can count as checks.
- **Hidden Walls**: If enabled, items behind hidden walls are shuffled.
- **Sols**: Different amounts of Sols act as filler items.

## Which items can be in another player's world?

Any shuffled item can be in other players' worlds.

## What does another world's item look like in Void Sols?

Items appear as standard item pickups in-game.

## When the player receives an item, what happens?

When a player receives an item, it is added to their inventory. If it's a weapon or equipment, it can be equipped at a rest point.
