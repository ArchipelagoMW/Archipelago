# Ys I Chronicles

## Where is the options page?

The [player options page for this game](../player-options) contains all the options you need to configure and export a config file.

## What does randomization do to this game?

Items found in treasure chests, given by NPCs, and dropped by bosses are shuffled. Key items required for progression (keys, Books of Ys, quest items) can appear anywhere in the game world or in other players' worlds in a multiworld.

## What items and locations get shuffled?

All treasure chests, NPC gifts, and boss rewards are shuffled. This includes:
- Weapons (Short Sword through Flame Sword)
- Armor (Chain Mail through Battle Armor)
- Shields (Small Shield through Battle Shield)
- Rings (Power Ring, Shield Ring, Timer Ring, Heal Ring)
- Keys (Shrine Key, Prison Key, Darm Key, etc.)
- Quest Items (Books of Ys, Silver Harmonica, Hammer, Rod, etc.)

## What is the goal?

The default goal is to defeat Dark Fact at the top of Darm Tower. This requires collecting all 6 Books of Ys.
